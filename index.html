ABOUT US

A brief story about us, keep an eye till the end.

M/s OMER Agro International is a Lahore (Pakistan) based company and is engaged in manufacturing trading of agricultural machinery and implements since 1983. Omer Agro has never compromised on quality remaining always competitive in the market as result, company has sent unparalleled standards of business growth. After capturing the domestic market, company decided to enter into the International market in the year 1993, thus the creation of Omer Agro International.

OMER Agro International is responsible for overseas operation marketing. Main products are farm based agricultural machinery; in particular all range of related implements. It is making regular export to many countries and also looking forwards for new counter parts in the new market rest of the world.

A team of highly qualified, skilled professional workers, equipped with the most modern and sophisticated infrastructure, tools and machinery, is putting in the best efforts to make our products /services unmatchable. Under the dynamic leadership of executive officer of the company has infused missionary spirit in the staff which is our most valued asset.
